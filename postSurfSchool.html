Why Da Nang surf school is special ?

June 16, 2020 posted by Admin Nat

Da Nang Surf School surfing lessons are mainly at My Khe Beach, in front of temple Da Nang resort 4 star resort. Da Nang Surf School has chosen My Khe Beach as it best suits everyone, from learners to developing or advanced surfing skills. Da Nang Surf School provides all surfing equipment, including soft surfboards as well as rash vests for your personalized surf lessons. Our equipment is the best for beginners, are in perfect condition and applies to the regulations set by the ISA. Soft boards with soft fins for absolute beginners,. And it’s for rent as well! All you need to bring are board shorts or swimsuit and plenty of sun screen. Surfing Lessons take approximately 2 hours.

What to explore at Da Nang surf school?

1 session – Intro and Experience of surfing

3 session – For those who felt the good vibe of surfing

5 sessions – for people who are serious about continuing surfing

Stand up Paddle Lessions Stand up paddle (SUP has the advantage of being easy to learn to Stand Up on a Board). On the first day mostly everyone can stand up on the surfboard, paddle, and have fun too. It really is very exciting to SUP alone, with friends and / or family. SUP is universal, for all ages and physical conditions! We guarantee you will stand up on the first lesson. We provide: Sup lessons in flat water; SUP Tours for experienced paddlers.
